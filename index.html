Some foods in VietNam that you must try!!!

Bun Bo Hue

Description: A spicy and flavorful Vietnamese beef noodle soup orginating from the former royal city of Hue.

Main ingredient: Rice vermicelli, lemongrass, beef, fermented shrimp sauce, pork knuckles, pig blood curds.

Pho

Description: Traditional Vietnamese noodle soup.

Main ingredient: Rice noodles, beef, herbs, broth, garnish, aromatics and spices.

Cha gio

Description: Vietnamese crispy fried spring roll filled with meat and vegetables.

Main ingredient: Ground pork, rice paper, mushrooms, carrots, eggs, onions

Mi Quang

Description: A popular noodle dish from central VietNam.

Main ingredient: Rice noodles, meat, eggs, peanuts,fresh herbs,flavorful broth.

Banh Mi

Description: Vietnamese sandwich made with a crispy filled with meat, vegetables, and fresh herbs.

Main ingredient: Bread, meat, eggs, cucumber, chili, pickled carrot and daikon, cilantro.

Goi Cuon

Description: Fresh Vietnamese spring roll that is light, healthy, and usually eaten cold.

Main ingredient: Rice paper, shrimp, pork, rice vermicelli noodles, lettuce, and fresh herbs.

Banh Xeo

Description: Vietnamese savory crispy pancake that is golden and crunchy.

Main ingredient: Rice flour, turmeric, coconut milk, pork, shrimp, bean sprouts, green onions.

Banh Khot

Description: A popular Vietnamese street food is equal parts savory,addicting.

Main ingredient: Rice flour, coconut milk, turmeric, shirmp, green onions.

Banh Cuon

Description: Soft and thin Vietnamese rice roll is steamed and usually served warm.

Main ingredient: Rice flour, water, filling usually made of ground prok, mushrooms.
